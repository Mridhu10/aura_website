The Mamanuca Islands!

Welcome to our page on The Mamanuca Islands in Fiji! Here you will find the popular and recommended places to stay, visit and eat at in this stunning holiday location!

So, why should you visit Fiji? The islands offer crystal clear waters, palm fringed sandy beaches and live coral reefs. There are villages, great resorts to visit, snorkelling, swimming and diving available. Some information about the location:

  • Fiji consists of 333 islands, about 110 of which are inhabited.
  • Visitors mostly see the native Fijian side of Fiji but in fact only 57 percent of the population claim full Fijian ancestry. The majority of the remaining islanders are Indo-Fijians of Indian heritage but Chinese, Southeast Asians, European, and other Melanesians and Polynesians (from elsewhere in the Pacific) have settled here.
  • Fiji is known to have some of the best food in the South Pacific thanks to the Indian, Southeast Asian and Chinese influences blended with Melanesian staples like taro, tropical fruits, coconut, pork and seafood.
Accommodation

Accomodation

The Lomani Island Resort features a 7-km private beach, and has timber decks with spectacular ocean views. Guests enjoy free use of snorkeling equipment, bicycles, stand up paddle boards, windsurfers and glass bottomed kayaks. Facilities include a fitness centre, billiards table, restaurant and a 35-metre freshwater swimming pool. Adults only.

At Malolo Island Resort you will find golden sandy beaches, coral reefs and coconut palms. Enjoy access to a swimming pool, 3 restaurants and 3 bars. Nadi is 28 km from the resort. Set amongst tropical gardens, the beautiful plantation-style bures on at Malolo Island Resort each have vaulted ceilings and full or partial sea views. There is an en suite bathroom, a verandah, refrigerator in each accommodation type and both beach towels and daily housekeeping are provided.

Overlooking a sandy private beach, Tokoriki Island Resort boasts a day spa, an outdoor pool and 2 restaurants. It offers bures and villas with a private patio and amazing ocean views. Tokoriki Island Resort Fiji is located on a secluded island 35 km off the west coast of Nadi. Nadi International Airport is a 15-minute flight away, and Port Denarau is 1 hour away by boat.

Tourist attractions and activities

Tourist attractions and activities

Malamala Beach Club is surrounded by Fiji’s famous crystal clear waters, and welcomes you to the world’s first beach club located on it’s very own island, just 25 minutes from Port Denarau. Experience one of the beach club’s day passes and enjoy white sand beaches, poolside day beds, beachside cabanas with butler service, an infinity edge pool, and amazing ocean views from Fiji’s best located island. Malamala Beach Club offers a delicious a-la-carte menu from light bites to tame your craving or pacific inspired meals and shared platters, an array of hand crafted cocktails and a club like atmosphere with laid back tunes mixed all day long.

Rainbow hued coral gardens, towering pinnacles and plunging drop offs - outstanding diving on the doorstep...1200 Species of fish, exotic Fijian endemics and fast moving sharks and game fish. Over 500 types of corals, cobalt seas and sheltered coves...All adding up to unforgettable experiences, whether for the seasoned scuba diver or the first timer. Tokoriki Diving is an award winning PADI 5 Star Dive Centre located at the beachfront of Tokoriki Island Resort. They offer a great variety of diving for certified divers as well as a full range of PADI courses and introductory dives. They offer daily diving to divers aged 16 years and above staying on Tokoriki Island.

Jet Ski Island Adventures offer the most thrill-seeking tours around the Mamanuca Islands! They have a range of different tours designed to suit everyone's taste for adventure. Suitable for all ages, families, groups, couples and individuals. Explore the beautiful islands and zoom over the crystal clear waters of Fiji.

Amenities

Proper Costa Rican food in a secluded outdoor restaurant is what is offered at La Cuchara del Buen Sabor. WIth very friendly and helpful staff, this restaurant is a great place to eat, especially with a wide variety of fresh seafood options.

Want some good Costa Rican food at a reasonable price? Then Soda Pura Vida is the perfect place for you. The restaurant offers delicious food for fair prices and is close to Santa Teresa beach, which makes it perfect for people going to the beach.

Pizza X Metro is an Italian pizza restaurant in a scenic area. The pizzas are known to be fantastic, you get a beautiful view from the restaurant, the staff are very kind and cater to your needs and overall, a fantastic experience.
