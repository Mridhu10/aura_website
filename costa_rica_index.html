Costa Rica!

Welcome to our page on The Nicoya Peninsula in Costa Rica! Here you will find the popular and recommended places to stay, visit and eat at in this stunning holiday location!

So, why should you visit Costa Rica? One reason that it is a must-visit place is to see the lifestyle that brings about an impressive life expectancy of 79 years. The Nicoya Peninsula is specifically known for longevity (and also has a fantastic beach!). Some information about the country:

  • The constitution of this peace-loving nation have forbidden a standing army since 1949 and is now home to the United Nations University for Peace.
  • The country is very biodiverse, hosting 5% of the world’s biodiversity while only taking up 0.03% of the world.
  • Costa Ricans have some quirky greetings - Instead of hola (hello), Costa Ricans say “Pura vida” (pure life). Costa Ricans also refer to their partners as “media naranja”, which means the other half of their orange.

Accomodation

The Tango Mar resort is the perfect place for a family stay, with many activities and an outdoor swimming pool, facing the beach. It is rated 4.5 stars and is at a cheap price which makes it perfect for families whatever the size!

The Casa Chameleon was built with couples in mind, so if you're looking for a quiet tropical honeymoon, this is the best place for you. With beautifully decorated rooms, the hotel gives off an aura of tranquility and is a relaxing place to stay

The Pranamar Villas and Yoga Retreat is ideally located on a pristine beach on the Pacific Coast of the Nicoya Peninsula. Lush, tropical surroundings, year-round surfing, and warm sunshine bless each moment while inviting you deeper into your practice. This is an intimate nine villa community designed with a tropical Asian and Costa Rican flair, including a chlorine-free pool.

Attractions

Perfect for animal lovers, the Cabo Blanco Absolute Natural Reserve is a great place to visit and have a relaxing walk. You can learn about the various species of animals and wildlife, walk on the trail through the reserve and also go to the beach nearby.

The Casa Chameleon was built with couples in mind, so if you're looking for a quiet tropical honeymoon, this is the best place for you. With beautifully decorated rooms, the hotel gives off an aura of tranquility and is a relaxing place to stay.

The Ylang Ylang is a calm, secluded beach for anyone who wants a bit of peace and quiet. There is easy access to the beach and the hotel is in a quiet residential area. Looking for a place to relax and de-stress? This is the right one for you.
